Imperial Isle

the crown jewel of Wish Island Maldives – is a destination where elegance, serenity, and nature converge. Formerly known as Kaalhehutta Island, this 20-acre paradise is reimagined as a realm of regal beauty in the heart of the Gaafu Dhaalu Atoll. Its name reflects a commitment to offering guests a truly majestic experience, surrounded by turquoise waters, white sands, and lush greenery. With 10 acres dedicated to refined tourism and hospitality, the island features exclusive overwater villas, private beach bungalows, and immersive experiences inspired by royal island living. The remaining 10 acres of Imperial Isle are devoted to sustainable agriculture, highlighting a balance between luxury and environmental harmony. From farm-to-table culinary offerings to wellness treatments infused with local botanicals, every element is crafted with purpose. Whether seeking seclusion, adventure, or celebration, guests will discover a sanctuary designed for those who desire more than just a getaway – they’ll find a timeless retreat where nature and nobility coexist.
