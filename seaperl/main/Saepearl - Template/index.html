WHY CHOOSE US

Nestled in the pristine Gaafu Dhaalu Atoll, the Wish Islands are a collection of four enchanting islands – Kaalhehutta, Villigaala, Bolimathaidoo, and Ukuruhutta – each offering its own unique charm. From lush tropical landscapes and crystal-clear lagoons to world-class resorts and thrilling recreational activities, the Wish Islands combine natural beauty with luxurious comfort. Whether you seek relaxation, adventure, or unforgettable family experiences, the Wish Islands promise a dream escape in the heart of the Maldives.

Kaalhehutta Island

Where Tranquility Meets Purpose

Kaalhehutta Island, part of the exclusive Wish Islands in the Maldives, spans 20 acres of natural beauty and intentional design. What sets Kaalhehutta apart is its harmonious integration of tourism and sustainability — with 10 acres dedicated to eco-conscious hospitality and the remaining 10 acres focused on organic agriculture. Guests are invited to experience island luxury while connecting with nature through farm-to-table dining, serene landscapes, and wellness-inspired experiences. The design philosophy prioritizes privacy, simplicity, and a deep respect for the environment, making it an ideal retreat for those seeking peace, authenticity, and purposeful travel. At the heart of Kaalhehutta is our identity: sustainable luxury with a soul. We focus on creating meaningful guest experiences rooted in nature, culture, and wellbeing. Whether you’re staying in a beachfront villa, exploring tropical gardens, or enjoying a quiet sunrise yoga session, Kaalhehutta offers a refined yet grounded atmosphere. It’s more than a destination—it’s a lifestyle of balance, where luxury supports sustainability, and every moment is designed to inspire inner calm and connection.

Villigaala Island

The Heart of Entertainment and Adventure

Villigaala Island is the vibrant entertainment hub of the Wish Islands, spanning 27.18 acres and designed to ignite energy, excitement, and connection. With 50% of the land dedicated to world-class recreational and leisure facilities, Villigaala is home to a one-of-a-kind Mini Formula Track, a Horse Riding Trail, Bowling Alleys, Baseball and Archery Centers, a VR Zone, and an Arcade bursting with both classic and modern games. Whether you're racing, riding, or relaxing, every corner of Villigaala is built to thrill. What defines Villigaala is its bold spirit and commitment to family-friendly fun and premium leisure. Designed to bring people together, the island offers unforgettable experiences for all ages — from kids' zones and thrilling rides to sports complexes and waterfront adventures. The 30-room, two-story hotel at the island’s center provides comfortable, stylish stays for families, couples, and groups looking to blend excitement with relaxation. Villigaala isn't just a place to stay — it’s a destination to play, explore, and make memories that last.

Ukuruhutta Island

Intimate Luxury in a Natural Sanctuary

Covering 12.36 acres, Ukuruhutta Island is the most intimate and exclusive of the Wish Islands, offering a private, nature-immersed escape. With 50% of its land reserved for boutique tourism development, the island is carefully curated to host luxurious beachfront villas, private bungalows, and tranquil hideaways that blend seamlessly into the tropical surroundings. Surrounded by turquoise waters and lush vegetation, Ukuruhutta is designed for guests seeking solitude, serenity, and a deep connection to the natural beauty of the Maldives. Ukuruhutta’s identity lies in its focus on exclusive privacy and refined simplicity. Unlike larger, busier resorts, it offers a quiet, personalized experience with a strong emphasis on wellness, barefoot luxury, and environmental harmony. Whether it’s a romantic getaway, a spiritual retreat, or a digital detox, Ukuruhutta delivers a calm, restorative environment where time slows down and nature takes the lead.
