Your Shoreline Sanctuary

Each of our 52 Beach Villas is positioned along the powder-soft coastline, offering uninterrupted views of the turquoise lagoon. No two villas are identical — every design draws inspiration from different elements of island life, whether it’s coral patterns, coconut palms, or wave rhythms. Designed for Comfort, Crafted for Uniqueness

Personalized Interior Themes

Each room and villa at Kaalhehutta is uniquely designed, blending coral-inspired art and local craftsmanship. No two interiors are alike — each tells a distinct story shaped by wind, water, flora, and heritage, creating a truly one-of-a-kind stay.

Private Outdoor Spaces

Each accommodation offers a private outdoor space—garden terrace, beach balcony, overwater deck, or ocean-view plunge pool—crafted for peaceful moments, scenic views, and a deep connection to nature.

Luxury Bedding & Linens

Guests enjoy restful nights with plush linens, organic duvets, and pillows tailored for comfort, all in soothing island-inspired tones of sand, coral, reef, and sky.

Rain Showers & Freestanding Tubs

Bathrooms are private sanctuaries with rain showers, deep soaking tubs, and in select rooms, open-air layouts offering serene ocean or garden views.

Smart Entertainment Systems

Each room includes a smart TV, streaming access, Bluetooth speakers, and charging stations—blending comfort with modern convenience.

Curated Mini-Bar

Our mini-bars are stocked with local snacks, herbal teas, tropical fruits, fine wines, and daily refreshed gourmet treats.

Daily Housekeeping & Evening Turndown

Rooms are serviced twice daily, with evening turndown featuring a treat or local touch like shells or herbal infusions for a comforting end to your day.

Complimentary High-Speed Wi-Fi

Enjoy seamless, high-speed internet in your room and across the island — perfect for work, streaming, or sharing your moments anytime.
