Every Stay, A Unique Story

At Kaalhehutta Island, we believe that no two journeys should feel the same — and that philosophy is reflected in every corner of our accommodation design. From oceanfront villas to elevated hotel stays, every space is thoughtfully crafted to offer a distinct ambiance, a personalized experience, and a lasting connection to the natural beauty and spirit of the island.

52 Signature Beach Villas – Your Shoreline Sanctuary

Each of our 52 Beach Villas is positioned along the powder-soft coastline, offering uninterrupted views of the turquoise lagoon. No two villas are identical — every design draws inspiration from different elements of island life, whether it’s coral patterns, coconut palms, or wave rhythms. Guests enjoy direct beach access, spacious indoor-outdoor living, private garden decks, and exclusive curated interiors that tell a different story in every villa.

25 Bespoke Bungalows – Immersed in Island Tranquility

Tucked within tropical greenery or placed near quiet coves, our 25 unique bungalows are havens of peace and comfort. Each is individually themed — from artisanal Maldivian craftsmanship to modern rustic elegance — and offers spacious verandas, cozy indoor lounges, and open-air showers that invite nature in. Whether for couples, creatives, or solo seekers, each bungalow offers a one-of-a-kind atmosphere tailored to restful rejuvenation.

10 Floating Haven Villas – Luxury on Water, Inspired by Nature

Drifting gently above the clear lagoon, our 10 Floating Villas redefine luxury with a sense of weightless serenity. Each villa features its own design signature — one may have a glass-bottom floor panel to watch marine life glide beneath, another may feature a private infinity plunge pool with a sunrise view. Inspired by ocean currents, coral reefs, and marine elegance, no two Floating Villas offer the same perspective or style, but all deliver the soothing embrace of the sea.

>
100 Distinct Hotel Rooms – Elevated Island Living

Spread across a beautifully designed 3-story hotel, our 100 rooms are crafted to provide variety, character, and comfort. Each floor offers a different experience: garden-view retreats, ocean-facing sanctuaries, and top-floor panoramic hideaways. Room interiors vary in theme — from wave-motif suites and sand-toned decor to floral-inspired havens and cultural contemporary rooms. The hotel includes family rooms, couple’s retreats, and work-and-play suites — ensuring no repetition, and no generic stay.

Personalized Interior Themes

Every room and villa at Kaalhehutta is intentionally unique. From coral-inspired art pieces to furnishings that reflect local craftsmanship, no two interiors are the same. Each space tells its own visual story, inspired by the island’s elements — wind, water, flora, and heritage — offering guests a truly one-of-a-kind atmosphere that enhances the sense of discovery.












Private Outdoor Spaces

All accommodations feature private outdoor areas — be it a shaded garden terrace, an overwater deck, a beach-facing balcony, or a plunge pool with panoramic ocean views. These spaces are designed for privacy, serenity, and connection to nature, offering perfect settings for morning coffee, evening sunsets, or midnight stargazing.












Luxury Bedding & Linens

Guests sleep wrapped in comfort with plush, high-thread-count sheets, organic cotton duvets, and pillows chosen for both support and softness. Patterns and colors are inspired by the tones of the island — sand, coral, reef, and sky — giving each room its signature sleep environment.












Rain Showers & Freestanding Tubs

Bathrooms are indulgent retreats in themselves, with walk-in rain showers that simulate tropical rainfall and deep soaking tubs, many of which are placed to offer ocean or garden views. Select accommodations feature open-air bathrooms or outdoor tubs, allowing you to bathe surrounded by nature in total privacy.












Smart Entertainment Systems

Each room is equipped with a flat-screen smart TV, offering access to international channels, on-demand movies, and popular streaming platforms. Bluetooth speakers and device-charging stations are included for your convenience, making it easy to enjoy both technology and tranquility.












Curated Mini-Bar

Our in-room mini-bars go beyond the basics — featuring artisanal Maldivian snacks, herbal teas, infused waters, and a handpicked selection of fine wines and premium spirits. Healthy options, tropical fruits, and gourmet treats are refreshed daily, ensuring delight in every detail.












Daily Housekeeping & Evening Turndown

Rooms are serviced twice daily to maintain impeccable comfort and cleanliness. Evening turndown includes a small treat or locally inspired token — such as handmade shells, botanical notes, or a bedtime herbal infusion — designed to offer warmth and care at the close of your day.












Complimentary High-Speed Wi-Fi

Seamless, fast internet is available throughout your room and the entire island, allowing guests to stay connected when they wish — whether for work, entertainment, or sharing their experience with the world.












Climate Control

Each accommodation features eco-efficient, individually adjustable air-conditioning and ceiling fans. The system maintains a perfect temperature for relaxation while being environmentally conscious, supporting our sustainability values.












Personalized Welcome Gift

Every guest receives a welcome gift tailored to the occasion — a honeymoon memento, a birthday token, or a wellness-themed starter kit. This thoughtful gesture adds a personal touch to your arrival and sets the tone for a memorable stay.












24/7 In-Room Service

Discreet and responsive, our in-room service team is available at any hour. Whether you’re craving a midnight dessert, need extra pillows, or want to book a last-minute spa treatment, assistance is just a call away — always delivered with genuine care and island hospitality.
